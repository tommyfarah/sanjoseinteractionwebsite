This is the San Jose Mineta International Airport

The San Jose Mineta International Airport (SJC) is the second busiest airport in the Bay Area after the San Francisco International Airport (SFO) and is ideal for travelers in the south bay and from the Santa Clara Valley that prefer not to go to SFO due to the distance. Its proximity to downtown San Jose means there are no skyscrapers allowed to be built in the city. It has domestic flights to other large cities in the United States and international flights to Mexico and Japan.
